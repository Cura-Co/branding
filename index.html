Voice & Tone

How CURA Speaks

Confident but not arrogant. Direct and clear. Results-led, not hype-led. Always pointing toward a next step.

📣
Marketing
Bold, benefit-led, and empathetic to the pain of admin overload. Name the problem clearly before the solution. Lead with outcomes and data — hours saved, ROI percentages.
⚙️
Product
Clean and functional. Labels are short. Metrics are prominent. The UI does the talking — minimal explanatory copy. Every word earns its place.
💬
Support
Helpful, patient, and human. We guide rather than lecture. Speak to the business owner, not the developer. Emphasise ease — "done for you", not "DIY".

Key phrases

AI Automation, Done Right Results-Focused Implementation Real Results, Proven ROI Your Data, Protected Up to 6 hours saved per day

Always avoid

  • Overly technical AI/ML terminology without context
  • Hype language ("revolutionary", "game-changing") without backing data
  • Passive voice when describing what CURA does
  • Jargon that alienates non-technical SME owners

AI & Agents

Using CURA with AI

The brand-identity.json is machine-readable by design. Point any AI agent, LLM prompt, or automation at it to get consistent brand output without manual copying.

✍️
Copywriting
Feed the voice & tone section to get on-brand marketing copy, emails, and social posts without briefing the AI from scratch each time.
🎨
Design & UI
Pass color tokens and typography scale to any design or front-end agent to generate pixel-accurate components that match the system.
🔍
Brand Review
Ask an agent to audit any content or UI against the JSON — it can flag off-brand language, wrong colors, or missing key phrases.
🤖
Agent Pipelines
Fetch the JSON as a tool call or system context so multi-agent pipelines always have the latest canonical brand data at runtime.
